ONYX E-CELL
About

Onyx is the Entrepreneurship cell of one of the most premier institutes in India, The National Institute of Engineering Mysuru. It was started by a group of enthusiastic final year students in 2009 and has become one of the model E-cells in India. Onyx aims at developing entrepreneurial attitude in the minds of students through hands-on workshops, seminars, activities, festivals and events. It is also a member of the National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN). It has grown in leaps and bounds since its inception Onyx is a recipient of various major awards like NEN E-WEEK Championship, Nilma Rovshen Creativity Award, Best Public awareness award. Onyx is a 3 time back-to-back winner of NEN E-WEEK CHAMPIONSHIPS. In the years to come,Onyx hopes to create a new league of entrepreneurs and make our country proud.
